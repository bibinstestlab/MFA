MFA: Enhancing Your Account Security

This guide outlines how to set up and use Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) to enhance the security of your email account. MFA adds an essential extra layer of protection by requiring you to verify your identity using more than one method, even if your password becomes compromised.

Why is MFA Important?

In today's digital world, robust security is crucial. MFA significantly reduces the risk of unauthorized access to your account and sensitive information. By requiring a second form of verification, it helps protect both your data and the overall integrity of our environment.

Available MFA Methods:

  • Microsoft Authenticator App: A mobile application that provides push notifications or verification codes for secure login.
  • Mobile Verification (SMS/Phone Call): Receiving a verification code via a text message (SMS) or a phone call to your registered mobile device.

    • Tap "Scan a QR code".
